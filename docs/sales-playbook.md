# Sales Playbook

## The Offer

**"We install an AI system that brings you customers every month."**

NOT "We build websites"

## The Call Script

When Harpoon AI calls:

```
"Hey [Name], I put together a quick website preview showing how 
[Business] could get more calls locally. 

It takes 30 seconds to look at — want me to text it to you?"
```

If hesitant: "No pressure. I can just text it over."

If interested: "Cool, I'm sending it now. Take a look and we can chat later."

## The Follow-Up

After they see the demo:

**Email/SMS:**
```
"Hey [Name], did you get a chance to look? 

Most businesses like yours are losing money online every day because:
- No website OR
- Website doesn't rank on Google OR
- Website doesn't turn visitors into calls

I can fix all of that. We handle everything monthly so you focus on running the business.

Want to chat for 15 minutes this week?"
```

## The Close

When on the call:

**Don't pitch features. Show ROI:**

"You're currently getting [X] calls/month. Our system can help you get [X+20%] calls/month.

That's worth [$ amount] to you.

We charge $[monthly price]/month to handle all this for you.

Sound worth it?"

## Pricing Presentation

**Option A:**
- "We can build you a website for $500-$1,000 one-time."

**Option B (recommended):**
- "Or we manage everything for you for $250-$400/month. Most of our clients choose this because you actually get results."

**Always go with Option B first.**

## Objections & Responses

**"I already have someone doing this"**
"Cool, are they delivering results? Most contractors end up with us because they get 20-30% more calls."

**"It's too expensive"**
"If it brings you 5 more calls/month at $500/call, that's $2,500/month in revenue. How does $350/month sound?"

**"I'll think about it"**
"I get it. When would be a good time to follow up? [Set specific day]"

## Follow-Up Sequence

Day 1: Demo sent
Day 3: Follow-up email (if no response)
Day 7: Phone call
Day 14: Final offer email

If no response after day 14, move to nurture list.

## Upsells

After 30 days, offer:
- Advanced SEO ($100-$200/month extra)
- Paid ads ($200-$500/month)
- Multi-location expansion ($300-$500/month)

Positioning: "Your website is bringing calls, now let's amplify it."
